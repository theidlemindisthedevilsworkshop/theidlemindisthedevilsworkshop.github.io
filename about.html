What this is.

This site is a small institution for a common failure mode: using intelligence as insulation.

When you can think well, you can delay well. You can explain your delay. You can build a mythology around your delay. And then you can call it “discernment.”

This workshop tries to break the spell with a simpler demand: name what you’re doing.

What this is not

  • Not productivity advice.
  • Not therapy.
  • Not a brand promise.

What this is

  • A mirror.
  • A gag that keeps escalating into a thesis.
  • A permission structure: do less honestly, do more imperfectly.
